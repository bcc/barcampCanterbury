Description

Unconference

BarCamp Canterbury is an unconference that is completely free-to-attend and based loosely around the theme of technology.

As an unconference, the content of BarCamp Canterbury is provided by you, the attendees! Meet with like-minded people, share your ideas and engage in discussions.

If you you have a topic youâ€™d like to present, pick a timeslot on-the-day to give a talk.

Previous Events

BarCamp Canterbury has run since 2008.

Events host between 80 and 160 attendees.

The University of Kent School of Computing has hosted the event for over 10 years and provided immense support in fostering our community.

Activities

Talks 💬

On the day, we will share a link to an editable online timetable grid of 30min slots.

If you'd like to give a talk, you simply add the details of the talk to the grid.

Share your passion for anything that you've built, made, tinkered or engaged with.

  • (Virtual) BarCamp Canterbury 2020- Talk Grid
  • BarCamp Canterbury 2019 - Talk Grid
  • BarCamp London 2019 - Talk Grid.

Talks tend to be tech-related but they can be about just about anything.

Attendees don't have to give a talk, although we do encourage everyone to get involved in some way.

You don't need a ppt or much of a plan; Just passionately talking about your stuff is enough.

All event-rooms will have projectors with HDMI connectivity and visualisers.

Theme

Each year we have a loose fun theme.

This year; Are you ... a wizard? 🧙

Free Food! 🍕

Thanks to our generous supporters, lunch and snacks will be provided!

(As this is a free event we may not be able to provide food for all dietary requirements)

Swap Shop ↹

Do you have any unloved/unwanted gadgets, cables, tools, boards, tech, bits, stuff?

Donate any working bits that someone else could use in an upcoming projects.

One persons junk is another persons treasure!

After Event 🎉

After the main event there will be a social gathering nearby. More details will be announced closer to the date.
